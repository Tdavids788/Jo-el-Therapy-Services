ABOUT US

At Jo-el Therapy Services, we understand that managing health challenges while balancing work and family commitments can be overwhelming. As experienced physiotherapists, we priotize creating tailored fitness routines that support long-term health, strenght and well-being.

Our Programs are designed to fit seamlesly into your busy schedule year-round. We offer a variety of therapeutic services, including Aerobic exercises, Yoga and Massage Therapy for both adults and children.


If you are feeling Overwhelmed by stress, fatique or anxiety, our therapy sessions are designed to restore balance and guide you towards a state of relaxation and comfort.


We specialize in therapeutic services for children with conditions such as Cerebral Palsy, Autism and other health challenges. One of our key services is Brain Gym, which icludes specialized exercises such as:

  • Thinking Cap: Enhances attention, Memory retention and cognitive processing.
  • Posiitive Point: Boost energy, confidence and promotes calmness.
  • Brain Tap: Encourages clear, relxed thinking
  • Brain button: Activates the brain's attention system for improved focus.
  • Oral Exercises: Strengthens muscles in the mouth and face, improving speech and oral health (including tongue, jaw, lips, cheek exercises and bubble blowing).
  • Massage Therapy: Helps reduce muscle tension, improves circulation and increase flexibility.


Our Professional team is dedicated to providing you with personalized care to promote overall health and well being
