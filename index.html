ABOUT US

At Jo-el Therapy Services, we understand that managing health challenges while balancing work and family commitments can be overwhelming. As experienced physiotherapists, we prioritize creating tailored fitness routines that support long-term health, strength, and well-being.

Our programs are designed to fit seamlessly into your busy schedule year-round. We offer a variety of therapeutic services, including aerobic exercises, yoga, and massage therapy for both adults and children.

If you are feeling overwhelmed by stress, fatigue, or anxiety, our therapy sessions are designed to restore balance and guide you toward a state of relaxation and comfort.

We specialize in therapeutic services for children with conditions such as cerebral palsy, autism, and other health challenges. One of our key programs is Brain Gym, which includes specialized exercises such as:


  • Thinking Cap: Enhances attention, Memory retention and cognitive processing.
  • Positive Point: Boost energy, confidence and promotes calmness.
  • Brain Tap: Encourages clear, relaxed thinking
  • Brain button: Activates the brain's attention system for improved focus.
  • Oral Exercises: Strengthens muscles in the mouth and face, improving speech and oral health (including tongue, jaw, lips, cheek exercises and bubble blowing).
  • Massage Therapy: Helps reduce muscle tension, improves circulation and increase flexibility.


Our Professional team is dedicated to providing you with personalized care to promote overall health and well being
